The Introspect Experience is a unique summer experience designed for dancers 12+ who are interested in cultivating their artistic expression through collaboration, internal reflection, improvisation, and intention. Creator and Artistic Director, Megan Long, designed this program to inspire pre-professional dancers to value their inherent worth and discover what makes them a one of a kind mover, creator, and human.

The one week summer experience takes place in Charlotte, NC at Open Door Studios. Daily classes include:

  • Contemporary, Modern, or Ballet technique
  • Improvisation
  • Composition/Choreography
  • Partnering
  • Wellness/Dance Kinesiology
  • Professional Prep/Marketing
  • Meditation/Gratitude Practice

A professional photo and video shoot are included in the experience to help dancers begin to build their artistic portfolio. The week concludes with a public informal showing of choreography built by the dancers in collaboration with Megan.
