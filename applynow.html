Applying for the Introspect Experience!

The Introspect Experience is a summer intensive open to all dancers ages 12+ held at Fusion Dance Company (5770 Bethelview Rd Cumming, GA 30040) July 14th-18th, 2025.

Led by Artistic Director, Megan Long, this week is dedicated to growing your artistry, creativity, and purpose as a dancer. Technique classes, composition/choreography classes, improvisation classes, and wellness classes will be offered. A professional photo shoot, video shoot, and final performance are included in the experience. Guest faculty include Noelle Dave, Jes Huber, and Sarah Stokes. Dancers from all studios and locations are encouraged to apply.
